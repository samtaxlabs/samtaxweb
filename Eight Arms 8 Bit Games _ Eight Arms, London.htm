How to play

Triple jump

Keyboard

  • Run  =  Any key
  • Hop  =  Spacebar
  • Step  =  Spacebar
  • Jump  =  Spacebar

Touch

  • Run  =  Tap the left of the screen
  • Hop  =  Tap the right of the screen
  • Step  =  Tap the right of the screen
  • Jump  =  Tap the right of the screen

Pro tip: If you don't hit space soon enough after landing you'll trip, if you hit it exactly as you land you'll get a height boost. Good Luck!
